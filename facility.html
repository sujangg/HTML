WHY BESS

BESS is responsive to the changing programs of educational delivery, and provide a physical environment that is comfortable, safe, secure, accessible, well illuminated, well ventilated, and aesthetically pleasing. BESS consists of not only the physical structure and the variety of building systems, such as mechanical, electrical and power, telecommunications, security. The facility also includes furnishings, materials and supplies, equipment and information technology, as well as various aspects of the building grounds, namely, athletic fields, playgrounds, areas for outdoor learning, and vehicular access and parking.

MONTESSORI BASED

Montessori methodology of instruction is adopted to form clear concept from initial stage of learning. Real life situation is created in order to provide real feeling to the young learners.

TRANSPORTATION FACILITY

We have comfortable and accessible transport facilities for needy students. Almost half of the total students are using BESS school buses and van.

INTERACTIVE PARTICIPATION

Practical lessons are the backbone of effective teaching learning. Up-to-date labs are set up to conduct practicals.
