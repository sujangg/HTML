INTRODUCTION OF BESS

Bhaktapur English Secondary School is a renowned school for unique and innovative educational practices established in the year 2049 B.S. It has got tranquil environment away from hustle and bustle city life. Number of leading educational institutions: Curriculum Development Centre, National Examination Board, TITI, CTEVT, UGS etc are next to this school as close neighbours.It has laid strong foundation with sound goodwill in the community. BESS believes that learning takes place in a happy, caring and stimulating environment in which every stakeholder has to play important role for achievement of common goal.
