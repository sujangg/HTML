Message From Principal

Bhaktapur English Secondary School is a students’ friendly school. The students of this school are serving in various sectors of social life. Scientists, medical doctors, engineers, civil servants, nurses, social workers, businessmen, teachers etc are the real treasures of the nation and the glory of BESS. 25 years of outstanding record of performance has made compulsion to extend the GRADE XI and XII. We are confident to continue the legacy of BESS with your kind support and collaboration.

MONTESSORI BASED

Montessori methodology of instruction is adopted to form clear concept from initial stage of learning. Real life situation is created in order to provide real feeling to the young learners.

EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Extra Curricular Activities are carefully scheduled to expand the horizon of learning. They are planned to develop the physical, mental and social development. The students have gained remarkable achievements.

PRACTICAL & INTERACTIVE PARTICIPATION

Practical lessons are the backbone of effective teaching learning. Up-to-date labs are set up to conduct practicals.
